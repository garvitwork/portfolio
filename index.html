My Journey

My journey with AI started from a love for mathematics and problem-solving during my undergrad at Delhi University. While exploring numbers and theories, I discovered how they connect with machine learning and computer science, which pushed me to dive deeper into AI.

  • I've built impactful projects — from an income prediction app (91%+ accuracy) to a bias detection web app that promotes fairer AI.
  • I presented my research on AI-driven stock prediction at an international conference, combining theory with real-world impact.
  • During my internship at FastHire, I developed predictive models for hiring and forecasted gold prices, turning ideas into practical solutions.
  • Today I blend math, research, and hands-on AI engineering, focusing on systems that are accurate, ethical, scalable, and impactful.

What Drives Me

  • Mathematical clarity: using math (optimization, linear algebra, statistics) to design robust models and principled solutions.
  • Fairness-first ML: researching and engineering bias-mitigation techniques that preserve accuracy while improving equity.
  • End-to-end impact: shipping reproducible pipelines and web apps (Streamlit, MLOps tooling) so models actually help users and stakeholders.
  • Data-driven storytelling: turning complex analyses into clear insights that decision-makers can act on.
  • Continuous learning & collaboration: presenting research, iterating with peers, and integrating tools like DagsHub / MLflow for reproducible work.
