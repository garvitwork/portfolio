01 Data Engineering · BI · ML

AEGIS — AI-Driven Planetary Defense Intelligence Platform

End-to-end data engineering + AI + BI system that prioritizes which near-Earth asteroids need urgent re-observation, using real NASA/JPL data, a MySQL star schema, Isolation Forest anomaly detection, and an interactive Power BI drillthrough report.

Why it matters

Telescope observation time for tracking near-Earth objects is scarce and expensive. AEGIS combines impact risk, orbital uncertainty, and orbital anomaly into a single prioritized watchlist for planetary defense teams.

02 Demand Forecasting · Retail Analytics

AI-Powered Retail Demand Forecasting & Inventory Optimization

End-to-end pipeline predicting product-level demand for a real UK-based online retailer and flagging restock risk — built with Python (XGBoost), a MySQL storage layer, and a Power BI dashboard with 20+ DAX measures and AI visuals.

Why it matters

Retailers routinely lose revenue to stockouts and overstock when reorder decisions are made on gut feel. This pipeline replaces that with a demand-driven forecast-to-decision workflow, reaching 87%+ forecast accuracy on real transactional data.

03 Churn Prediction · CLV · Power BI

AI-Powered Customer Retention & Revenue Intelligence Platform

Predicts customer churn, estimates customer lifetime value, and surfaces AI-driven retention insights on the Olist Brazilian e-commerce dataset (~100K real orders) — built with Python (Random Forest, sentiment analysis) and Power BI, with an optional MySQL warehouse layer.
